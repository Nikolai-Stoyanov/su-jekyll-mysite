Admission requirements

Proof of the vaccination

Please note that pets who do not have up to date vaccinations will not be allowed into the hotel and reservations made regardless of these requirements will have to be paid in full by the owner.

Additional requirements

Ensure your pet regularly has flea and tick treatment as well as deworming treatment

Please note that any vats comming to the hotel must be brought in secure a cat cage or carrier and all dogs on a leash.

Assessment & temperament

Al pets who will be staying with us will under go a screening process to determine their temperament and social behaviors

Sterilization requirements

All male dogs to be neutered at 8 months and ALL female dogs to be sprayed at 6 months before visiting us.
